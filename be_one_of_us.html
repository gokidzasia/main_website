Commitment & Understanding

By signing this form, I commit to using my God-given talents for the ministry’s mission of expanding His Kingdom.

I agree to:

  1. Fulfill my volunteer duties with dedication, excellence, and teamwork.
  2. Use my gifts to glorify God, contributing to the Great Commission (Matthew 28:19-20).
  3. Attend scheduled meetings and events, honor deadlines, and remain open to feedback.
  4. Serve with humility, understanding that this commitment is for the Lord’s purpose and glory.
